Welcome to the Secret Garden Getaway!

Are you looking for a beautiful place to relax and enjoy delicious breakfast?

Your experience here will be worthwhile. We provide a cozy greenery environment with a variety of breakfast options suitable for your liking.

Kitchen

The ingredients used in the kitchen are the highest-quality to ensure every meal is fresh and tasty. Fruits are picked at the right ripeness, farm-fresh eggs are used, as well as homemade bread. Our selection of ingredients are not only high-quality, but they are also natural.

Local farms and producers have helped our service provide satisfaction and purity to customers just like you.

Seating Area

The Secret Garden Getaway seating area is surrounded by a lush garden with a variety of flowers, plants, and trees. Enjoying breakfast while gazing at all the plant life is recommended.

The variety of flowers we grow attract beautiful insects like butterflies and ladybugs. The Texas state butterfly, the monarch butterfly, can even be spotted here!

Dietary Restrictions

Let us know if you have any dietary restrictions or allergies before ordering from our kitchen!

If your diet consists of gluten free, vegetarian, or vegan, we can always provide a special meal for you to enjoy.
